# Responsible Artificial Intelligence

TheProspector uses artificial intelligence to improve research efficiency, data organization, and scouting workflows.

AI is implemented as an assistive technology designed to enhance the work of hockey professionals—not replace their expertise, judgment, or decision-making.

---

# Our Philosophy

The platform follows a simple principle:

> **Artificial Intelligence assists. Human intelligence decides.**

Every AI-assisted capability is intended to support experienced scouts, coaches, analysts, and hockey operations staff while preserving human oversight throughout the evaluation process.

---

# Objectives

AI capabilities are developed to:

* Reduce repetitive research tasks
* Improve information organization
* Accelerate player discovery
* Enhance analytics
* Surface meaningful patterns
* Improve productivity without reducing transparency

---

# Current AI Applications

Artificial intelligence may assist with:

## Player Enrichment

Enhancing player profiles using publicly available or authorized information sources.

---

## Data Organization

Improving consistency across player records, statistics, and scouting information.

---

## Search Assistance

Helping users locate relevant players, leagues, teams, and scouting information more efficiently.

---

## Analytics Support

Highlighting trends and relationships within prospect data to assist hockey operations staff.

---

## Workflow Assistance

Supporting administrative tasks that allow scouts to spend more time evaluating players.

---

# Human Oversight

AI-generated information should always be reviewed by qualified personnel.

The platform is intentionally designed so that:

* Scouts make final evaluations.
* Coaches make development decisions.
* Management makes organizational decisions.

AI never replaces professional hockey expertise.

---

# Transparency

Where practical, AI-assisted functionality should be understandable to users.

The platform aims to provide:

* Clear descriptions of AI-assisted features
* Understandable outputs
* Reviewable recommendations
* Opportunities for human verification

---

# Fairness

The Prospector is developed with the goal of supporting objective and consistent evaluation processes.

Development efforts seek to reduce unintended bias while recognizing that no automated system can fully capture every aspect of player development.

Human context remains essential.

---

# Privacy

AI features are developed in alignment with the platform's Privacy Policy and Security practices.

User-generated scouting notes, organizational information, and private evaluations are treated with appropriate safeguards.

See:

* **PRIVACY_POLICY.md**
* **SECURITY.md**

# Limitations

Artificial intelligence has limitations.

AI-generated information may:

* Contain incomplete information
* Miss important player context
* Require verification
* Change as new data becomes available

Users are encouraged to validate important information before making hockey operations decisions.

---

# Continuous Improvement

AI capabilities are continuously evaluated and refined.

Future improvements will focus on:

* Better explainability
* Improved transparency
* Higher quality recommendations
* Greater user control
* Enhanced workflow integration

---

# Ethical Development

The Prospector is committed to responsible AI development through:

* Human oversight
* Transparency
* Accountability
* Privacy by design
* Continuous evaluation
* Practical engineering

These principles guide the evolution of every AI-assisted capability introduced into the platform.

---

# Commitment

Artificial intelligence should help hockey professionals make better-informed decisions—not make those decisions for them.

TheProspector is committed to building AI that is practical, understandable, responsible, and always in service of the people who know the game best.
