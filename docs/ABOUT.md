# About The Prospector

The Prospector is a modern hockey intelligence platform developed to support player discovery, scouting research, analytics, and hockey operations.

Originally conceived as a private scouting advantage, the platform has evolved into a professional software system capable of organizing hundreds of thousands of player records while providing meaningful intelligence through analytics, visualization, and AI-assisted workflows.

---

# The Story

The Prospector began with a simple question.

**How can scouts spend less time searching for information and more time evaluating talent?**

Traditional scouting often requires collecting information from numerous sources, comparing statistics manually, and organizing large amounts of research.

The Prospector was created to bring those workflows together into a single platform.

Instead of replacing scouts, the platform exists to make experienced hockey professionals even more effective.

---

# Why It Exists

Modern hockey organizations generate enormous amounts of information.

Player statistics.

League movement.

Development history.

International competition.

Scouting observations.

The challenge is no longer finding information.

The challenge is organizing information into actionable intelligence.

The Prospector was built to solve that problem.

# Platform Philosophy

The platform is guided by several core principles.

## Human First

Technology should support experienced scouts—not replace them.

Final evaluations remain the responsibility of qualified hockey professionals.

---

## Data With Context

Statistics alone never tell the complete story.

The Prospector combines structured information with visualization, enrichment, and human insight to help users better understand player development.

---

## Responsible AI

Artificial intelligence assists with organization, summaries, enrichment, and workflow efficiency.

AI is intended to improve productivity while remaining transparent and reviewable.

---

## Continuous Improvement

The platform evolves through practical engineering, user feedback, and incremental releases.

Every version is intended to make scouting faster, clearer, and more effective.
