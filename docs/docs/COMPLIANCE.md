# Standards & Compliance

TheProspector is developed with a commitment to responsible software engineering, privacy, security, transparency, accessibility, and continuous improvement.

This document describes the governance principles that guide the design, development, deployment, and operation of the platform.

The objective is to build software that organizations can trust through thoughtful engineering practices and clear operational standards.

---

# Compliance Philosophy

Compliance is viewed as an ongoing engineering process rather than a one-time milestone.

Platform policies, documentation, operational procedures, and technical safeguards are continuously reviewed as new capabilities are introduced.

Our guiding principles include:

* Transparency
* Accountability
* Responsible development
* Privacy by design
* Security by design
* Continuous improvement
