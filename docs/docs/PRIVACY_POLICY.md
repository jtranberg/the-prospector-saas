# Privacy Policy

TheProspector is committed to protecting user privacy while providing a professional hockey intelligence platform for scouting, player evaluation, analytics, and research.

This Privacy Policy describes the types of information the platform may process, how that information is used, and the principles guiding responsible data management.

---

# Privacy Principles

The platform is developed around several core privacy principles.

* Transparency
* Accountability
* Data minimization
* Responsible data use
* User control
* Continuous improvement

Privacy considerations are incorporated throughout the platform design and development process.

---

# Information We May Collect

Depending on platform functionality and user configuration, the platform may process:

## Account Information

Examples include:

* Name
* Email address
* Authentication credentials
* Organization affiliation
* User preferences

---

## Platform Usage

To improve reliability and user experience, the platform may record operational information such as:

* Login activity
* Feature usage
* Search activity
* Saved searches
* Watch lists
* Application diagnostics

This information helps improve platform performance and usability.

---

## Scouting Information

Authorized users may create or manage:

* Scouting notes
* Player evaluations
* Rankings
* Tags
* Internal observations
* Watch lists

These records are intended to remain private to the organization or user that created them unless intentionally shared.

---

# Player Information

The Prospector organizes hockey-related information obtained from publicly available sources, licensed providers, and user-generated content.

Player profiles may include:

* Name
* Position
* Nationality
* Team
* League
* Statistics
* External profile links
* Enrichment data

The platform does not claim ownership of third-party content.

---

# Cookies and Sessions

The platform may use cookies or similar technologies to:

* Maintain user sessions
* Remember preferences
* Improve usability
* Support authentication
* Enhance platform performance

Future releases may expand cookie management options.

---

# How Information Is Used

Information is processed to:

* Operate the platform
* Support scouting workflows
* Improve search performance
* Generate analytics
* Deliver AI-assisted features
* Maintain platform security
* Improve user experience

Information is not processed for purposes unrelated to the operation of the platform without appropriate authorization.

---

# Information Sharing

The Prospector does not sell user information.

Information may only be shared when necessary to:

* Operate platform infrastructure
* Support authorized integrations
* Comply with legal obligations
* Protect platform security
* Support user-requested services

---

# Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to:

* Provide platform functionality
* Support organizational workflows
* Meet operational requirements
* Comply with applicable legal obligations

Retention practices may evolve as platform capabilities expand.

---

# User Rights

Where applicable, users may request:

* Access to their information
* Correction of inaccurate information
* Export of eligible data
* Deletion of eligible account information

Requests are reviewed in accordance with applicable laws and operational requirements.
